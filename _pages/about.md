## 👋 About Me

I am a PhD researcher in Mechanical Engineering specializing in **nonlinear vibration energy harvesting**.

My research focuses on extracting energy from **flow-induced vibrations**, including:
- Galloping
- Vortex-Induced Vibrations (VIV)

I apply **analytical and numerical methods** to model nonlinear systems and improve energy harvesting performance.
